SAVE LIVES FOUNDATION
HELPS REFUGES AND VICTIMS
OF NATURAL DISASTERS



We do everything possible and impossible so that
everyone can get the help they need.

The Save Lives, donation and support foundation
operates in africa only. Our volunteers visit
the areas affected by the natutal disasters to
help people. We deliver humanitarian aid and
find new homes for refugees.

Save Lives has been operating since 2025 and
provides fundraising and goods collection,
procument, and humanitarian aid to the right spot.
