OUR MISSION

Everyday tens of millions of people find themselves in a different situation
due to natural disasters such as Floods, landslides,earthquakes and other natural disasters.

The Save Lifes Donation Support Foundation has a mission to provide maximum assistance to those in need.
Here are some efforts made by our donation in the past years to provide food,medical amenities
and shelter to those suffering from natural disasters in Africa.

NOVEMBER 2023 LANDSLIDE IN CHAD:
N"DJAMENA

In November 2023,landslide swept through the country of Chad,

We aknowledged the landslide and sent a team of doctors to help with the landslide and water
crisis. Our team provided medical care,food and shelter to those affected by the landslide.
Our team also provided clean water to the affected communities.

WE RAISED A TOTAL OF TWO HUNDRED DOLLARS
